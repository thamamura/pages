Anger management — how effective are these strategies?

Rank these strategies from most to least effective for reducing anger. Use the arrows to reorder. You can also add strategies your group comes up with.

  1. Scream into a pillow
  2. Watch something funny
  3. Hit a punching bag
  4. Talk about it (after calming down)
  5. Write about your anger in a journal
  6. Play or watch competitive sport
  7. Express anger to the person who caused it
  8. Take a deep breath
  9. Go for a jog
  10. Count to 10
Use the arrows to reorder from most effective (top) to least effective (bottom).
